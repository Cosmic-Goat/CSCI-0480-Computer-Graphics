I've always been interested in digital art, especially exploring how the application of mathematics (via programming and engineering) can be used to express an artistic vision, or to illustrate or explain a concept. I've explored different applications of computer graphics, such as 3D modelling and animation, game development, and virtual reality. In fact, I've actually wanted to take this course since before I even joined NYU, so you could say I'm a tad excited.
